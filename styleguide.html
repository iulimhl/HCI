5. Icon Style

  • Minimalist line icons are used across all interfaces.
  • Icons are paired with text labels for clarity.
  • Important states use color: green for allowed, orange for active, coral for limited access.
  • Rounded shapes make the interface feel friendly and easy to use.

6. Spacing and Layout Rules

  • Consistent spacing based on an 8px grid system.
  • Large rounded cards are used for main actions and device controls.
  • Dark cards sit on a warm neutral canvas background.
  • Soft shadows and blur effects separate interactive elements from the background.
  • Important actions are placed in large touch-friendly areas.

7. Platform Adaptation

Platform Design Focus
Mobile phone / Tablet Dashboard overview, room navigation, device control and household activity.
MR Headset Spatial panels, gaze-based selection, gesture control and room-aware overlays.
Smartwatch Quick routines, glanceable status, minimal controls and fast confirmations.

8. Accessibility Principles

  • High contrast text for readability in dark environments.
  • Large interaction zones for users with limited computer experience.
  • Color-coded permissions combined with text labels, not color alone.
  • Simple navigation patterns repeated across all devices.
  • Clear feedback after every interaction, such as active states and status messages.
